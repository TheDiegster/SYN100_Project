Welcome to your new adventure! Inspired by Animal Crossing: New Horizons, Stardew Valley, and Pokemon, Seeds of Life is a hypothetical farming simulator game that aims to educate others on the climate crisis by demonstrating how the reduction of it can help bring about a strong community and flourishing ecosystem.
